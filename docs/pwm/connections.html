Dear Brave Family,

Okay, so, slight change of plans: I’ve been captured.

By what, you ask? Oh, nothing too alarming… just large, hairy creatures that smell like a mix of wet dog, burnt toast, and regret. They’re definitely not human. Beyond that, I’m still figuring it out.

They don’t seem particularly violent—just really confused. Honestly, I think they tried to solve my puzzles but, uh… let’s just say “logical reasoning” isn’t exactly their specialty. They’ve been staring at the puzzles the way I stare at my phone when it refuses to unlock because of a smudge.

Since they can’t figure out the puzzles, I’m hiding clues inside them. If you’re reading this, it means you’re my best hope!

Please rescue me!,

Crack the puzzles, follow the clues, and find me before I become the centerpiece of some hairy banquet or get shipped off to a mystery destination I do not want to discover.

No pressure, though. You’ve totally got this. Probably.

Sincerely,
Randy
